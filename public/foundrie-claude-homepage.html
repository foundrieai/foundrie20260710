LaunchCode: From idea to exit. Five phases, continuous support.

Every venture moves through the same forge. Ideation and Validation find the idea worth building and prove the market before you overbuild. Then five execution phases, from Problem-Solution Fit to Maturity and Exit-Readiness, carry it forward - while your Evidence Vault and Portfolio Map run through all of it. Progress is always earned through evidence, flowing like molten metal from first heat to final form.

Enter the journey >
IdeationFind the idea worth building.
→
ValidationProve the market before you build.
→ The five phases begin
Phase 01

Problem-Solution Fit

Confirm a real, urgent, and valuable problem, and a solution concept a specific segment actually wants.

Phase 02

Product-Market Fit

Prove users return voluntarily, reach value quickly, and would miss the product if it disappeared.

Phase 03

Go-to-Market Fit

Find the repeatable channel, message, pricing, and sales motion that create predictable revenue.

Phase 04

Growth & Scale-Up

Turn the repeatable motion into durable scale across team, operations, product, and unit economics.

Phase 05

Maturity & Exit-Readiness

Build the governance, financial, and category evidence that creates real strategic options.

Evidence VaultThe interview notes, behavioral proof, metrics, quotes, and files that back every decision.
